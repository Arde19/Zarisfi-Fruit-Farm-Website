About Us

Zarisfi Fruit Farm is a newly established family partnership founded in 2025 and based at Mains of Drum, Aberdeenshire.

Our business was created to provide fresh, high-quality strawberries directly to local customers and visitors. By selling straight from the farm, we offer fruit with full freshness, traceability, and competitive value.


The farm is run by brothers Ardeshir Zarisfi and Aryamehr Zarisfi. Aryamehr leads the practical farming work including planting, crop care, harvesting, and maintenance. Ardeshir manages planning, finance, compliance, and administration while also supporting day-to-day work on-site.

  • Family-run and locally focused
  • Fresh produce sold directly from the farm
  • Simple, honest, and sustainable operation
  • Committed to quality and customer experience

Our Strawberries

We specialise in outdoor-grown strawberries, carefully produced and hand-harvested for direct sale during the summer months.

🍓 Freshly Picked

Our strawberries are harvested at peak ripeness to deliver the best taste, colour, and freshness.

🌱 Grown with Care

We use practical, cost-effective growing methods including protective growing sheets to help manage weather and quality.

🧺 Sold Directly

Fruit is packed into tubs and sold directly on-site, reducing unnecessary transport and keeping produce fresher for longer.

Strawberry Cambridge Favourite A Bare Root

Cambridge Favourite is a classic variety known for its reliable cropping and balanced flavour. It offers a pleasant natural sweetness with a gentle, traditional strawberry taste that many people recognise and enjoy. The fruit is juicy, aromatic, and ideal for customers looking for a familiar, fresh strawberry with a soft texture and well-rounded flavour.
